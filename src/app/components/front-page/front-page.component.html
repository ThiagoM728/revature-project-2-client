Career Fairs Made Easy!

Fair Breeze is a career fair management system that makes it easier for students to view and learn about the companies attending a certain career fair. Companies can create profiles, add job listings and RSVP for career fairs hosted by Universities. Students on the other hand are able to view all job listings and save the job listings that they are interested in. Students are also able to filter the job listings making it easier to search for jobs that fit their needs.
